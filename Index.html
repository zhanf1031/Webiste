About Sichuan Cuisine

Sichuan cuisine, known for its bold and spicy flavors, is one of the most famous cuisines in China. It features a variety of dishes that use ingredients like chili peppers, garlic, and Sichuan peppercorns.

Featured Dishes

Kung Pao Chicken

A classic Sichuan dish made with diced chicken, peanuts, and vegetables, all stir-fried with a flavorful and spicy sauce.

Mapo Tofu

A spicy and aromatic dish made with soft tofu set in a spicy chili and bean-based sauce, typically cooked with minced meat and Sichuan peppercorns.

Sichuan Hotpot

A communal meal where diners cook a variety of ingredients like meat, vegetables, and tofu in a simmering pot of spicy and flavorful broth right at the table.
